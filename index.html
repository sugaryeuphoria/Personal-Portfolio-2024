About Me

In the tapestry of my childhood, I unwound the threads of curiosity, weaving intricate patterns of discovery with my very first computer that dad got us in the fourth grade. The luminous screen became my portal into the enchanting realm of binary secrets, sparking a journey into the inner workings of science and technology. Those early hours, consumed by a voracious appetite to unravel the mysteries of the digital universe, forged an unbreakable bond with the world of computers.

Now, as a Computer Science student, I carry those childhood sparks, translating them into a commitment to continuous learning and contributing meaningfully to the world we live in. This narrative encapsulates my journey from a curious child to a dedicated tech enthusiast, showcasing my unwavering passion for science and technology.
